# 🏥 Smart Health Surveillance and Early Warning System

An AI-powered web application developed to detect, monitor, and help prevent water-borne disease outbreaks in vulnerable communities through health data analysis, outbreak prediction, and real-time alerts.

## 📌 Overview

The Smart Health Surveillance and Early Warning System is designed to strengthen public health monitoring by enabling the early detection of water-borne disease outbreaks. The system collects health-related information from local healthcare workers, community volunteers, and health centers, analyzes the data using Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning techniques, and provides timely alerts to health authorities.

The platform also supports water quality monitoring, hotspot visualization, and decision-making through an interactive dashboard, helping officials respond quickly and allocate healthcare resources effectively.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Detect potential outbreaks of water-borne diseases at an early stage.
- Collect and analyze health data from multiple community sources.
- Predict disease trends using AI and Machine Learning.
- Support water quality monitoring and contamination reporting.
- Generate real-time alerts for healthcare authorities.
- Visualize disease hotspots through interactive dashboards.
